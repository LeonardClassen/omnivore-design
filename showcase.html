Omnivore Design System
one token layer · two surfaces

One brand, two surfaces.

A single framework-neutral token layer (tokens.css) drives both the customer-ops cloud dashboard (light) and the industrial HMI on the WAGO panel (dark). Flip the switch above — every swatch and component below re-themes from the same tokens. Nothing here is hard-coded.

01

Brand & the two greens

Green is the one company accent. But a green button must never read as an "OK" status on the dark panel — so brand and status are two different green tokens, split by role.

Interactive
--brand
Primary CTAs · focus rings · selected/active · links · brand marks. Never status.
Status
--ok
"Running / healthy / present" — LEDs and status pills only. Never interaction.
Warm brand-green vs cool status-emerald are deliberately distinct hues. On the dark theme the gap widens (brand #4caf50 · ok #34d399) so the two never blur together.
